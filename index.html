About Us

Dear devotees, It is with the divine grace and blessings of GURU that the sacred task of building a temple for Shakti Traya Sree Lakshmi Sree Saraswathi Sree Rajarajeswari has fallen on us. We are privileged to be bestowed with the honour of embarking on such a noble yet onerous task.

  • A serene place of worship dedicated to Goddess Saraswati, Rajarajeswari, and Lakshmi.
  • Established in [2009], it serves as a spiritual haven for devotees.
  • Located in [Madanayakanahalli, Hanumanthe Gowdana Palya, Makali, Karnataka 562162], easily accessible for visitors.

"Our temple is not just a place of worship but a vibrant hub of community activity. We offer a variety of programs and services, including cultural events, educational workshops, and charitable initiatives, to support and uplift our community."
